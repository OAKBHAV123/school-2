School Activities

In our endeavor to make Oakridge International School a great learning institute, we constantly seek feedback and act on it. In the recently concluded Parent Survey, we have received feedback from some parents that they would like to have a paid lunch service at school on a rainy day. We understand that there could be days, due to certain exigencies, when as parents we cannot support our children with the daily lunchbox. To help such parents we have introduced a facility to be able to have school lunch on those odd days. This paid lunch facility allows home lunch students who on rare occasions would like to have school lunch, to pay and avail the school lunch.
